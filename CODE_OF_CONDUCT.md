# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment for our community include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful and professional.
- Constructively sharing feedback.
- Collaborating with empathy and transparency.
- Respecting people with different backgrounds and perspectives.

Examples of unacceptable behavior by project participants include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.
- Abusive, derogatory, or exclusionary language.
- Personal attacks or demeaning comments.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Deliberate misrepresentation of identity or work.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying expected behavior and will take appropriate action in response to behavior that violates this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you are subjected to or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening a GitHub issue with the title `CODE_OF_CONDUCT_REPORT`.
